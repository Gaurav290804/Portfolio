The Best Movies According to Gaurav

My top 3 movies of all time

Dhurandhar

A gritty, high-intensity film centered around power, patriotism, and covert operations, highlighting the dark side of ambition and sacrifice.

Zindagi na Milegi Dobara

A feel-good road-trip movie about friendship, self-discovery, and living life fearlessly, set against stunning travel backdrops.

Bhaag Milkha Bhaag

An inspiring biopic of Milkha Singh that showcases grit, discipline, and the relentless pursuit of excellence against all odds..
